Our story

Because of Covid-19, people suffered physically and mentally. The virus disrupted our schedule and caused us to seek a different way to live our lives. Many people (including us) struggled with finding the right tempo again. Our team wanted to take the first step to help people get back on the right track!

So we worked hard with our community to create an app that helps people develop a routine and seek advice on any health-related topics. We listen to their feedback and keep our product accessible and responsive. With testings and revisions, we will continue to improve our product for the people who need love and support.

Our Mission

"What it means to be alive"

These are the words we follow when helping others. We ask out community what they cherish the most in life and what we can do to accentuate it. Your physical and mental well-being is our number one priority and we will do our best to help you achieve them.

Start a
routine

Whether you are struggling with post-graduate life, unemployment, or depression, creating a routine will help you get your life back on track. You can plan out your day and your device will give you reminders throughout the day. Your mood and energy level changes day by day. You can cater your schedule based on tasks you need to complete and feel accoplished at the end of the day.

Finding
advice

Some people do not know where to start or seek to further their understanding of health. We have health professionals write and review articles for you. These articles contain years of research and data while presented in a cleaner format. You can read the growing collection of advice and save them at any moment!

All the sleep
you need

Consistent sleep schedules and insomnia are the most common sleep issues people face. When you insert your sleed data, the product can pinpoint the problem and provide excellent solutions to optimize your sleep.
